Our Salon & Spa

What to Expect

E. Claire Salon strives to deliver you a one-of-a-kind, tailored, and relaxing experience with us. We offer two Stylist Islands, a Color Bar, a Make-Up Bar, two Nail Tables, two Pedicure Chairs, and a luxurious Spa Oasis.

As you enter E. Claire, you'll be offered coffee or fresh fruit-flavored water. One of our stylists or practicioners will discuss how to best cater to your needs.

Special Occasions

E. Claire recognizes that prom night and weddings are important events in your life, and is fully set up to cater to your special occasion needs. We offer hair, make-up, and nail services, as well as relaxing massages to get you ready for your big event.
