What We Do

E. Claire Salon is a Redken precision salon and spa for men and women designed to serve you. We are here to help you feel beautiful, tranquil, and rejuvenated.

Salon Services include hair design, Redken color design, and healthy hair treatments.

Skin Repair include signature facials, Bioelements facials featuring Bioelements products, and advanced treatments.

Body Repair feature various modalities (spa massage, deep tissue work, craniosacral massage, and myofascial release) and add-ons such as hot stones or Thai reflexology.

Other Services we offer include nail care, hair removal, beauty services, and hair and make-up for special occassions.
